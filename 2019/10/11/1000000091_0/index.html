多多练习位置词：

inside & outside

（里面 & 外面）

例句：The dog is inside/outside the dog house.


例句：The woman is inside/outside the car.

over & under

（在 ... 之上 & 在 ... 之下）

 

例句：There is a girl riding a bike over the bridge. There is a boy riding a bike under the bridge.


例句：The butterfly is over the flower. The ladybug is under the flower.

 

up & down

（上/向上 & 下/向下）

 

例句：The boy on the seesaw swings up and the girl swings down.


例句：The smaller cat is up in the tree and the bigger cat is down under the tree.

 

以上只是一些简单的小例子
