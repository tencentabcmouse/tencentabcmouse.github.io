Ham with Jam?

火腿加果酱？

 

Pam is a girl.      

Pam 是一个女孩。

 

Pam likes jam.   

Pam 喜欢果酱。

 

Sam is a boy.

Sam 是一个男孩。

 

Sam likes ham.

Sam 喜欢火腿。

 

One day Pam said to Sam, “Will you eat some of my jam?”

有一天，Pam  对Sam说：“你愿意尝尝我的果酱吗？”

 

Sam said he would not eat jam! 

Sam说他不喜欢吃果酱！

 

Pam said she would not eat ham!

Pam说她不喜欢吃火腿！

 

“I have a plan,” Pam said to Sam.“Maybe jam tastes good on ham!”

 “我有一个主意，” Pam对Sam说：“把果酱涂在火腿上也许很好吃！”

              

Pam took out her jar of jam.

Pam拿出她的一罐果酱。

 

She put some on a piece of ham.

她把果酱涂在一片火腿上。

 

“This  is good,” Pam said to Sam.

“好吃，”  Pam对Sam说。

 

“Try  a piece of ham with jam.”

“尝一片涂果酱的火腿吧。”

 

Sam put jam on top of ham.

Sam 在火腿上涂上果酱。

 

Then he ate his piece of ham.

然后他把他的那片火腿吃了。

 

Sam told Pam, “Now I like jam!”

Sam告诉Pam， “现在我喜欢吃果酱了！”

 

And Pam told Sam, “Now I like ham!”

Pam也告诉Sam， “现在我也喜欢吃火腿了！”

 

The End
